The Story

My heart sings happy songs.

For the last ten years, I've been walking into a children's home in Manzanillo, Mexico called Casa Hogar Los Angelitos. The kids there have been through more than most adults will face in their entire lives. And they compete at life with everything in them.

Watching them taught me what Shatter Expectations actually means. It's not a coaching slogan. It's the thing those kids do every single morning.

I bring the kids of Casa Hogar into every room I speak in — because they are the proof that mindset, heart set, and soul set aren't optional. They are the whole game.

The Framework

Five sets. One framework. Built in real gyms.

Audiences leave with three or four tools they can use Monday morning. Not theory. Tools.

Mindset

03

Soul Set

05
