About ASI

Three decades on the sideline. One mission: athletes who compete when it matters.

Kevin Skeens is a former D-2 athlete, a 30-year volleyball coach, and the founder of Ballistic Volleyball Club, which he started in 2010. His club teams went on to be nationally ranked and made multiple state tournament appearances — known for upsetting more talented programs through mental toughness.

He studied Sociology and Sports Psychology in undergrad and completed postgraduate work with the International Mental Game Coaching Association. He's a certified high school athletic director through the NFHSC. Today he serves as Director of Volleyball & Mental Performance Coach at Advance Sport IQ, and as Mental Toughness Coordinator at Wellington Volleyball Academy.

He's also Director of Development at TCF Cares, and has spent the last decade leading immersion trips to Casa Hogar Los Angelitos in Manzanillo, Mexico. The kids there are the reason the framework is called Shatter Expectations.

  • IMGCA Certified Mental Game Coach (postgraduate)
  • NFHSC Certified Athletic Director
  • BS Sociology & Sports Psychology
  • Founder Ballistic Volleyball Club (2010)
